FIFA World Cup

Teams

Argentina

Argentina has been runners up three times: in 1930, 1990 and 2014. In 18 World Cup tournaments, Argentina had 47 victories in 81 matches. The team was present in all but four of the World Cups, being behind only Brazil and Germany in number of appearances (with the 2022 edition included, to be played)

Brazil

Brazil is the most successful national team in the history of the World Cup, having won five titles, earning second-place, third-place and fourth-place finishes twice each.

Uruguay

As the inaugural winners of the World Cup and two-time champions, Uruguay has a proud footballing tradition
